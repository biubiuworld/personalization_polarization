Game 2 Instruction

Hello! This is Game 2 with 50 rounds.

At the beginning of the game, you will be randomly assigned an initial position and then in the following rounds, you will (1) observe two other subjects and decide whether to connect (or reconsider whether to keep the connection if they are already in your network) with them; (2) update your position based on your position and your neighbors' positions in previous rounds.
In Game 2, you will have 15 seconds to choose neighbors and 15 seconds to update position each round.

Your payoff in round \( t \) is as follows:

  1. If you have neighbor(s), your payoff this round is:
    Payoff this round = sum of payoffs from all neighbors
    And specifically, the payoff with one neighbor is:
    Payoff with neighbor \(k \) = \( \color{red}{250} \) - \( \color{red}{0.9} \) \( * \color{blue}D1 \) - \( \color{red}{0.1} \) \( * \color{blue}D2 \)
  2. If you donâ€™t have neighbor, your payoff this round is:
    Payoff this round = - \( \color{blue}D2 \)

\( \color{blue}D1 \) = (your position in current round \( t \) - neighbor \( k \)'s position in previous round \( t-1 \) )\( ^2 \)
\( \color{blue}D2 \) = (your position in current round \( t \) - your position in previous round \( t-1 \) )\( ^2 \)
