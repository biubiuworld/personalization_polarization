Practice Game Instruction

Hello! This is the 5-round practice game.

At the beginning of each game, you will be randomly assigned an initial opinion and then in the following rounds, you will (1) observe two other subjects randomly and decide whether to connect (or reconsider whether to keep the connection if they are already in your network) with them; (2) keep updating your opinion based on your opinion and your neighbors' opinions in previous rounds.
The goal of the game is to find agreements among all subjects. So you need to connect with subjects who have similar opinions.
In practice game, you will have 20 seconds to choose neighbors and 30 seconds to update opinion each round.

Your payoff in round \( t \) is as follows:

  1. If you have neighbor(s), your payoff this round is:
    Payoff this round = sum of payoffs with all neighbors \( * 10000 \)
    And specifically, the payoff with one neighbor is:
    Payoff with neighbor \(k \) = \( \color{red}{0.025} \) - \( \color{red}{0.2} \) \( * \) (your opinion in \( t \) round - neighbor \( k \)'s opinion in \( t-1 \) round)\( ^2 \) - \( \color{red}{0.8} \) \( * \)(your opinion in \( t \) round - your opinion in \( t-1 \) round)\( ^2 \)
